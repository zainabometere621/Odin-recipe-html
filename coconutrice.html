Coconut Rice Recipes

Odin Recipes

Descriptions

Coconut rice is a flavorful and aromatic side dish that pairs well with variety of cuisines, particularly Asian and Caribbean.

This recipe combines the creaminess of coconut milk with the simplicity of white rice, resulting in a deliciously rich and textured dish.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Minced garlic
  • Minced ginger
  • pepper mix
  • 1 small onion
  • 3 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • shredded coconut
  • 4 cups Sella basmati rice
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons chicken seasoning

Preparations

    Step 1: prepare the ingredients

  1. Rinse the rice: Rinse 4cups of Sella basmati rice under cold running water until the water runs clear. Drain well.
  2. Chop the onion: Finely chop 1 small.
  3. Prepare the garlic and ginger: Mince the garlicand ginger.

    Step 2: cook the Aromatics

  1. Heat oil: Heat some oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion: Add the chopped onion and fry until translucent.
  3. Add garlic and ginger: Add the minced garlic and ginger and fry for another minute.

    Step 3: Add the rice

  1. Add the rice: Add the rinsed rice to the saucepan and stir to coat the rice with oil and mix with the onion, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Cook for 1-2minutes: Cook the rice for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly .

    Step 4: Add the coconut milk and chicken stock

  1. Pour in the coconut milk and chichen stock: Pour in 3 cups of coconut milk and 1 cup of chicken stock.
  2. Add salt and salt and chicken seasoning: Add 1.5 teaspoons of salt and 2 teaspoons of chicken seasoning.
  3. Add pepper mix: Add pepper mix according to your taste.
  4. Stir well: Stir the mixture well to combine.

    Step 5: Bring to a Boil and simmer

  1. Bring to a boil: Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat.
  2. Reduce heat: Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the saucepan with a tight-fitting lid .
  3. Simmer: Simmer the rice for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.

    Step 6: Fluff and serve

  1. Turn off the heat: Turn off the heat and let the rice sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Fluff the rice: Fluff the coconut rice with a fork to seperate the grains.
  3. Garnish: Garnish with the shrdded coconut.
  4. Serve: Serve the coconut rice hot alongside your favourite dishes.
